## 4. Rebranding Opportunities
If you are planning a rebrand, consider the following pivots:
1.  **Lighter Theme**: Switch to a "Day Mode" (e.g., Waitrose Green/Cream) for a fresher, organic feel.
2.  **Minimalist Tech**: Remove the "Glass" and go for flat, high-contrast Swiss design (like Uber Eats).
3.  **Vibrant Pop**: Use bold, solid colors (Neon Yellow/Pink) to target Gen-Z specifically (like Gorillas/Getir).
